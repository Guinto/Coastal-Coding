An Introduction

Our Oxnard campus offers the best atmosphere of learning with its cool coastal temperatures and a perfect learning environment in a 7 Storey building.

We currently rent our classes from the National University which occupies both the 1st and 5th floors of the building.

As soon as you enter our classroom you are met by our professional and friendly staff, including the founder of the school, to give you a warm welcome. As expected, you will also meet your new colleagues who will soon become your teammates as you begin your three-month academic journey.

We carefully select students that have a strong passion for success. As long as you are intrinsically motivated to learn you will succeed in our bootcamp.
