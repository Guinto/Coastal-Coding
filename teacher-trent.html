Trent Ellingsen

Trent Ellingsen started programming when he was 10 by building websites for his family members.

He’s gone on to get a B.S. in computer science from California Polytechnic University. He’s worked as a Software Engineer for 5 years in both web and iOS at MINDBODY Inc. He’s held a management for 2 years during his time there and continues to develop on the side for fun. He has over 6 apps on the App Store including the MINDBODY App, Magic Drafter, Ship Ship Zoom, Photo Floof, and others.

He’s a passionate leader with over 8 years of experience leading and teacher others in various skills. He is continuing to grow and learn as an engineer to keep up to date with the latest technology. He’ll push you to excel and grow as a software engineer if you let him.
