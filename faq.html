Coastal Coding FAQ

What’s the student to instructor ratio?

The ratio is between 1:12 and 1:20

How many hours a week will I be on site?

At least 40 hours for full time students

How many hours are expected for the homework and projects outside of class time?

At least 40 hours, and this will still leave you with 8 hours of sleep per day.

How long will it take me to get a job after?

Allow yourself about three months of applying, interviewing and accepting a job.

Are there any refunds?

You will get a full refund at the end of the first day of class if the program does not suite your needs. By the end of the first week you will get a prorated refund. After the first week you will have invested too much time and energy to quit the program, therefore no refunds from week 2 - 12.

What is the criteria for selection?

We carefully select students that have a strong passion for success. The old saying goes, “Where there is a will there is a way.” As long as you are intrinsically motivated to learn you will succeed in our bootcamp and you will never regret your time with us.
