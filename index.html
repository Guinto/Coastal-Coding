What's so awesome about Coastal Coding?

We have put together a curriculum geared to meet what is on demand in the job market. Our curriculum is very flexible and responsive to the winds of change in the industry. We use textbooks to lay the foundation but we rely heavily on current trends. You will soon be familiar with online resources to enhance your knowledge while maintaining academic integrity, creativity and originality.

Why we are the best?

Never before have we such a vast and deep combination of passion, skill and talent as in our teaching faculty. Starting with the founder who has taught in three different countries and in two continents to some faculty with many years of industrial experience and managerial expertise. You will also meet frequently with mentors who work in the industry and also liaise with senior and junior colleagues from the different cohots. There is absolutely no need to look any further.

Why you would love Coastal Coding?

As soon as you enter our classroom you are met by our professional and friendly staff, including the founder of the school, to give you a warm welcome. As expected, you will also meet your new colleagues who will soon become your teammates as you begin your three-month academic journey.
